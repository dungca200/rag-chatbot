# Competitive Intelligence

Gather and analyze intelligence on competitors to inform strategic decisions.

## Workflow

1. **Identify targets** - Clarify which competitors or competitive landscape to analyze
2. **Gather intel** - Research public information, news, products, pricing, positioning
3. **Analyze** - Compare capabilities, strategies, strengths/weaknesses
4. **Deliver** - Present in requested format (report or brief)

## Research Approach

- Focus on publicly available information (websites, press releases, reviews, job postings, patents)
- Analyze product features, pricing, messaging, and customer feedback
- Look for patterns in hiring, partnerships, and investments
- Note information gaps and confidence levels
- Avoid speculation without evidence
- Prioritize actionable competitive insights
