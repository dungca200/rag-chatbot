# Market Research

Conduct comprehensive market research to inform strategic business decisions.

## Workflow

1. **Define scope** - Clarify the market, geography, timeframe, and specific questions
2. **Gather data** - Use web search, industry reports, and available sources
3. **Analyze** - Synthesize findings into actionable insights
4. **Deliver** - Present in requested format (report or brief)

## Output Formats

### Quick Brief (default for simple queries)

Use for fast decision-making:

```
# [Market] Brief

**Key Insight:** [One sentence takeaway]

**Market Size:** [TAM/SAM/SOM if available]
**Growth Rate:** [CAGR or trend]
**Key Players:** [Top 3-5 competitors]

**Opportunities:** [Bullet points]
**Risks:** [Bullet points]

**Bottom Line:** [Actionable recommendation]
```

### Structured Report (for comprehensive analysis)

Use when user requests detailed research or says "full report":

```
# [Market] Strategic Analysis

## Executive Summary
[2-3 paragraphs summarizing key findings and recommendations]

## Market Overview
- Market definition and scope
- Size and growth trajectory
- Key drivers and constraints

## Competitive Landscape
- Major players and market share
- Competitive dynamics
- Barriers to entry

## Customer Segments
- Primary segments and characteristics
- Needs and pain points
- Buying behavior

## Trends and Outlook
- Current trends shaping the market
- Emerging opportunities
- Potential disruptions

## Strategic Recommendations
1. [Specific, actionable recommendation]
2. [Specific, actionable recommendation]
3. [Specific, actionable recommendation]

## Sources
[List key sources used]
```

## Research Approach

- Start broad, then narrow based on user's specific needs
- Prioritize recent data (prefer sources from last 2 years)
- Cross-reference multiple sources for accuracy
- Distinguish between facts and projections
- Note data limitations and confidence levels
- Focus on actionable insights, not just data dumps
